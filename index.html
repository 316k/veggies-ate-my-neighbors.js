Veggies Ate my Neighbors

April 2017 : The evil agriculture corporation Monsinti runs an experimental program to crossbreed vegetables and nuclear weapons in order to generate more powerful plant seeds...

May 2017 : Monsinti's Scientists prematurely declares the experiment as a success and the resulting seeds are patented and sold to third-world farmers for a very cheap price.

January 2018 : Plants grown from Monsinti's patented seeds are used in the kitchen of a high-class vegan restaurant, and some customers mutate into crul and merciless chlorophyll-eating monsters.

By chance, you happend to have your own personnal reserve of meat-eating mutant plants that you bought in order to defend yourself in case of zombie apocalypse. Looks like it's time to use it !
